# Peek — Marketing landing (MagicPath)

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective Peek app users (iPhone), often arriving from social or App Store links. They skim quickly, want warmth and clarity, not spreadsheet anxiety.

## Product purpose

Explain why Peek is different from category-based budgeting apps: worth-it ratings, spending stories, humane plans.

## Brand personality

Playful, sticker-scrapbook, direct, anti-shame. Feels like the App Store product: purple + cream + orange mascot energy.

## Anti-references

Purple gradient hero text. Glassmorphism cards. SaaS hero metrics (big number + three stats). Cream/sand generic AI landing bg without brand intent. Uppercase section eyebrows on every block. Marketing buzzwords (seamless, empower, transform).

## Strategic design principles

- Design IS the product on this surface; commit to Peek palette and Baloo/Poppins/Caveat stack already in code.
- Interactive demos (worth-it, story reveal) must work with keyboard and reduced motion.
- Copy: specific, short, no em dashes.
